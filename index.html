Is sport important to us

What is sport?

Sport is a physical activity that is very important in everyday life.

How sport affects us?

Playing sports is very important because sports provide health to the body. By the way, we remove stress, we have better sleep and concentration.

How often you should play sports?

Sport is never enough! You should do sports often, it is recommended from 3 to 7 times a week.

A survey

We did a survey about sports. We asked a couple of questions and got answers.

We asked questions about whether the sport is important, whether you do sports, how often, which sport you prefer, whether you prefer outdoor or indoor sports. We received a lot of good answers, which makes us very proud and indicates that young people like to play sports.


Our reason for playing sports.


As we said before, sports are never enough. In addition to all the reasons about the health that sport provides us, you can also do sports out of love. You can have fun, you can meet a lot of friends. Sport is also good because of school, after every day you can relax and enjoy what you love.
